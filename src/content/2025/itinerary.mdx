# Itinerary

## Overview

- **Route:** Train from Sapporo to Fukagawa, ride north along the Sea of Japan coast to Wakkanai, ferry to Rishiri Island, then south along the Okhotsk coast, inland to Asahikawa, and back to Sapporo via Furano
- **Mode:** Self-supported cycle touring (no camping)
- **Season:** Late June through mid-July 2025
- **Accommodation:** Mix of guest houses, ryokan, onsen hotels, and Dormy Inns

---

## Route

### Starting Point

**Train from Sapporo to Fukagawa** — then ride north along the Sea of Japan coast toward Wakkanai.
