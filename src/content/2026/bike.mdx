# Bike Setup

## The Bike

**Surly Disc Trucker** — steel touring bike with disc brakes.

- **Tires:** Stock 700c x 41mm
- **Gearing:** Upgraded crankset for improved lower gearing (essential for Hokkaido's climbs)
- **Fenders:** Yes
- **Lights:** Yes (front and rear — essential for Hokkaido's frequent mountain tunnels)

---

## Bags

- 1x Ortlieb rear pannier
- 1x Ortlieb rear pannier/backpack (doubles as a daypack off the bike)
- 2x Ortlieb fork bags (front)

---

## Flying with the Bike

### Bike Case — Postcarry Transfer Case 150L
- Requires fork removal to pack
- Rolls up to backpack size when empty

### Rinko Bag (for trains/buses in Japan)
- Required for all train travel in Japan — bike must be fully bagged
- Costs 3,000-6,000 yen to buy in Japan
- Requires removing both wheels
- Lightweight and packable — can carry on the bike during touring
